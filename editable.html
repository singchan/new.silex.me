Own your data

While editing your site on silex.me your files are on your hard drive, available to Silex through a cloud service you trust or own: FTP, Cozycloud, Owncloud, Dropbox, Github and more to come.

Host your website anywhere

Your work on silex.me will result in an HTML page stored on your computer. Hit "publish" to optimize your site and host it anywhere.
